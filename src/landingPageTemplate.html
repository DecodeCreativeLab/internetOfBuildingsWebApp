In the wake of the global pandemic and in anticipation of the inevitable climate emergency, it is the perfect moment to radically redefine how we perceive, conceive and construct our urban environment. The Internet of Buildings studio combines computational urban design, data analytics, integrated simulation and interactive interfaces to understand and propose solutions for the future challenges of our cities.

Focusing on Barcelonaâ€™s Poblenou district, the studio works as a transdisciplinary project team and follows a multi-layered, multi-scalar and inter-temporal approach. The five major clusters of analysis (urban morphology, environmental ecology, energy exchange, mobility flow and social economy) are studied in different scales both in space (from building to districts) and in time (current, imminent and future challenges).

The Internet of Buildings studio is part of the Master in City & Technology course at the Institute for Advanced Architecture of Catalonia. Check out the Internet of Buildings syllabus or the MaCT website for more infomation!
